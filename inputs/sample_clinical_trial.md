# Clinical Trial Protocol Summary

## Study Title
A Phase III, Randomized, Double-Blind, Placebo-Controlled Study to Evaluate the Efficacy and Safety of Drug XYZ-2025 in Patients with Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus

## Protocol Number: CT-2025-0042
## Sponsor: PharmaResearch Inc.
## Principal Investigator: Dr. Emily Chen, MD, PhD
## Institution: Metropolitan General Hospital
## IRB Approval: IRB-2025-001234

---

## Study Objectives

### Primary Objective
To evaluate the change in HbA1c from baseline to Week 24 in patients receiving Drug XYZ-2025 compared to placebo.

### Secondary Objectives
1. Assess fasting plasma glucose (FPG) reduction at Week 12 and Week 24
2. Evaluate body weight changes
3. Monitor cardiovascular safety markers
4. Assess patient-reported quality of life (QoL) outcomes

## Inclusion Criteria
- Age 18-75 years
- Confirmed diagnosis of Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus
- HbA1c between 7.5% and 10.5%
- BMI 25-40 kg/m2
- Stable metformin therapy for >=3 months

## Exclusion Criteria
- Type 1 Diabetes Mellitus
- eGFR < 45 mL/min/1.73m2
- History of pancreatitis
- Active liver disease (ALT/AST > 3x ULN)
- Pregnancy or lactation

## Study Design
- **Phase**: III
- **Design**: Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled
- **Duration**: 24 weeks treatment + 4 weeks follow-up
- **Sample Size**: 500 patients (250 per arm)
- **Randomization**: 1:1 ratio, stratified by baseline HbA1c and BMI

## Dosing
- **Treatment Arm**: Drug XYZ-2025 10mg PO QD
- **Control Arm**: Matching placebo PO QD

## Safety Monitoring
- Adverse events recorded at each visit
- Liver function tests q4 weeks
- Renal function monitoring q8 weeks
- Cardiovascular events adjudicated by independent committee
- DSMB reviews at 25%, 50%, 75% enrollment
